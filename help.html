Induced Current Calculator

This project allows you to calculate the magnitude and direction of the induced current in a conductor based on its velocity and the strength of an applied magnetic field.
Input from User :- Turns in Solenoid , Length of Solenoid , Area of Solenoid(m^2) ,Resistivity of Solenoid(ohm-m),Pole Strength of Magnet(A-m), Length of Magnet(m), Velocity of Magnet(m/s),Distance of Magnet from Solenoid(m)
Output for User :- Magnetic Field (Tesla) , Induced EMF (V) , Induced current (A)
